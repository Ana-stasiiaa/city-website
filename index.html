Kyiv

Our chestnut capital. The city was founded in 482, has about 3 million hearts and is the 7th largest city in Europe. The widest and, at the same time, the shortest street in all of Europe - Khreshchatyk. The Kyiv metro is one of the oldest in Europe, and the Golden Gate station is one of the top 10 most beautiful in the world. From sunrises on the Singing Field, sunsets on the Dnipro to incredibly delicious pastries - everything here is so native.
